Welcome to Grand Prix Legends.

The year is 1967. Jack Brabham is Formula 1 World Champion and is partnered at his own team by Denny Hulme. Jim Clark is at the height of his racing powers and is partnered by former World Champion Graham Hill at Lotus. Future 3-times World Champion Jackie Stewart is racing for BRM. Another former champion, John Surtees, is driving for Honda. Bruce McLaren competes in his own car as does Dan Gurney in his All-American Racing Eagle. At Ferrari, Mike Parkes, Chris Amon and Lorenzo Bandini form another powerful line-up. Joachim Rindt, Jo Bonnier and Pedro Rodrigues all line up on the grid in Coopers.

Grand Prix Legends takes you to another era of racing, with the authentic feel of being a real race car driver and to the time where the man behind the wheel was more important than the machine and the driver was the one and only “on board computer” and a car’s delicate balance depended on the right-foot of a man brave enough to navigate tracks that wound their way through the forests, country sides and villages of post-war Europe, while reaching speeds in excess of 185 mph and while sitting in a fragile magnesium and aluminum tub filled with 40 gallons of highly volatile and exotic fuel and... no seat belts.

Now it is your chance to pit your racing skills against the true greats of Formula 1.
