

Daytona Continental 24 Hours - Daytona Beach ,Florida USA ver. 1.0 for
GPLegends GT MOD

Made by Ginetto and Sergio Loro.

Track Location: Daytona Beach FL-USA Track Type : Race Track Track
Length : 6.132km.- 3.81mi.  Track Num. Turns : 10 Track Year : 1967

Track Editor : Ginetto and Sergio Loro

--------------------------------------

INFOS:

This track was developed by Sergio Loro and myself, it reproduces the
1967 Daytona 24 hours.  Sergio started the project then passed it to
me to be revised and fine tuned, we did a lot of research to find out
how exactly was the layout in 67 and it is quite different than how it
started, I did my best to recreate it the most accurate as possible,
keeping an eye to the performance on the lower systems as mine is.
The track is a mandatory addon for the SportCar MOD.
