Zeltweg Airfield 1967 ----------------- 1.988 mile airfield circuit
used between 1958 and 1969, and was the site of the first Austrian GP
in 1964. It had a very bumpy concrete surface, and was superseded by
the new Oesterreichring in 1969.  -----------------
