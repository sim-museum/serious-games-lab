Time for a new track, and this time i am proudly present "Siracusa de
Sicily" or in english "Syracuse".  A few words before the first lap.
I don't know if you ever did an addon for gpl, it does'nt matter, but
please try to understand how difficult it is, to get informations
about a track from the 60's. And don't forget, it is a '67
non-championship track !  I have searched the whole www for infos and
pics of Syracuse, the result are 35 pics, 27 of them are from
http://www.latphoto.co.uk (thank you guys): 1 of the pits 1 of the
start 1 of the backstraight 1 ferrari 16 of corner 1 7 of corner 2.
So, with this huge amount of data, i started to create the track (have
i told i had 4 different pics of the layout?).

This is the result, and please don't flame me if there is a visitor
not on his original place (lol).

Installation: - Unzip "Syracuse_1.0.zip" and use Trackinstall.exe to
install - close your eyes and play Syracuse.wav to get the right
feeling for the track !  - run it


Performance: - my console: 1300 Athlon, 256 Mb Ram, GeForce2 Ti 450,
36 FPS in D3D and OpenGl in training mode 1280*1024, standart
installation - standart installation is the "Hires" version of the
track - because of the amount of trees, the track is really hardware
hungry - complete Glide compatible (mip-size), testet by me in OGL and
D3D without probs - recommend to set TossHighestMip = 0 in your
Core.ini - use OpenGl - dissable "Dynamic Lighting" in your grapfic
options (thanks to Jason) - there's a fps-help available
"syra_light.zip"
 

the AI: - not the best, but it's priceless

		

The Track: - located on the eastcoast of Sicily, near the city
Siracusa - lenght is 5566 m - lap times high 1.40 min
