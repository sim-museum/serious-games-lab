-------------------------------------- Historical Note:

During 1954  1966, the Nassau Speed Weeks were a high-octane end
of season play-off in the sun between Americans and Europeans
 serious racing and even more serious partying!  1966 was to see
the last of the Speed Weeks for the time being.  The World and Motor
Sport were changing rapidly and the racing season was extended
throughout the winter months in many countries.
