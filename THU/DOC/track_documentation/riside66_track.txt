Historical Note:

The road course opened on September 21st, 1957, and operated until
1988. The dragstrip operated from 1959 through 1970, and from 1983
through 1986.  All time statistics tracks fatal casualties all over
the world shows Riverside road course on the podium, just after
N³rburgring and Brands Hatch.  Now the track is gone for good; you
can see some pictures from 2001 of what remain back then here:
http://www.tamsoldracecarsite.net/RiversideToday.html
