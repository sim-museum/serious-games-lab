

55Mod Update Patch

55Mod Update Patch

ITEM 1: There are unique 3dos and mips for the Maser, Lancia and
Mercedes W196r to give the proper brake drum backing plates on the
front wheels.  Also, for the Mercedes W196s there are changed wheel
3do files to give the appearance of in-board drum brakes all around.
ITEM 2: The Gordini T16 has a graphics representation of the second
exhaust pipe and slightly larger suspension openings.  These include
the AI, player, default and all of the online carskins.  Some of the
darker blue carskins were left with the single exhaust, which is
carried over from some of the race-specific AI cars that are being
prepared.  ITEM 3: The Collins #36 car has the front grill trident
removed.  This is the '54 style chassis which the Owen Racing
Organization painted a darker green than the other British teams.
ITEM 4: Includes yellow #2 online Gordini.  ITEM 5: Includes alternate
brake temp fade indicator mip in the brabham car sub-folder. Cool
state for brakes is no longer 'green' but 'indicator off'.  ITEM 6:
Includes player car #4 of Moss's 1954 Aintree 200 Maser 250F.  ITEM 7:
Includes player car #4 of Pilette's 1954 Spa Gordini T16.  ITEM 8:
Changes to all car main 3do files to eliminate stub arms when 'no
driver arms' selected.  ITEM 9: Added Merc W196 online 1024x1024
carskin #40; added Maser 250F online 1024x1024 carskin #34.  ITEM 10:
Lighting angle on Connaught and Ferrari 625 mirrors adjusted.  ITEM
11: Ferrari 625 windscreen, dash and cockpit texture updates.  ITEM
12: Lancia D50 grill update. New Lancia D50 and Ferrari 555 mirrors.
ITEM 13: Tire textures given a more 3D shadowing.

Installation: Un-zip this archive into your GPL folder (or for
separate installs, your GPL55Mod folder) allowing over-writing of
files.  The 55Mod car files will be placed into the cars55
sub-folders.

Option: To use the alternate brake temp fade indicator, copy the
tempfade.mip file from the sub-folder in the brabham folder to the
brabham folder.

Credits: All carskins are made from the original 55Mod carskins
created by Royale, Shaun, MECH, sky, Border Reiver, francesco and
ginetto.  Fix to driver arms view for all cars was made by MECH.
Adjustment to Connaught and Ferrari 625 mirrors reflections was made
by hagapito40 - original textures by sky.  Ferrari 625 windscreen,
dash and cockpit texture updates were made by hagapito40 - original
textures by ginetto.  Lancia D50 grill update was made by hagapito40 -
original texture by sky.  New Lancia D50 and Ferrari 555 mirrors

The 55Mod Team hope that you enjoy these updates.
