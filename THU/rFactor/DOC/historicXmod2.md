Historic GT and Touring Car Mod for R-Factor 1 Study Guide
----------------------------------------------------------

Mod Overview
------------

*   Created by the historic modding team for the original R-Factor racing simulation
*   Features historic GT and touring cars from multiple eras (1950s-1970s)
*   Known for incredible depth, authentic handling, and period-accurate details
*   Requires GT Legends game to be installed alongside R-Factor 1

Key Technical Details
---------------------

*   Features period-accurate tire grip levels and slip angles
*   Includes authentic engine sounds recorded from real vehicles
*   Detailed damage modeling
*   Realistic weight transfer physics
*   Force feedback implementation called "real feel"

Vehicle Characteristics
-----------------------

*   Period-accurate handling behaviors
*   Significant weight transfer during cornering
*   Most cars require early turn-in and throttle management
*   Generally feature either understeer on entry or oversteer on exit
*   Different tire compounds affect grip levels based on era
*   Authentic engine sounds with mechanical and transmission noises

Historical Accuracy
-------------------

*   Accurate liveries from period racing
*   Correct engine specifications
*   Multiple variants of same model representing different racing series
*   Authentic interior details (like wood dashboards in British cars)
*   Period-correct modifications and body kits
