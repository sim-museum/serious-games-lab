### **Introduction to Chessmaster: Grandmaster Edition**
- **Overview**: Chessmaster is the most comprehensive chess software, updated with new features in the Grandmaster Edition.
- **Key Features**:
  - **12 Chess Variants**: Includes Marseillais, Progressive, Pocket Knight, Kriegspiel, and others.
  - **Correspondence Chess**: Play games without being online simultaneously with your opponent.
  - **Mini-Games**: Includes Fork My Fruit, Minefield, and Pathfinder.
  - **Chessmaster Academy**: Interactive lessons by IM Josh Waitzkin focusing on principles, positions, and experiences. Includes "The Art of Learning."
  - **Enhanced Chess Sets**: Includes 10 new designs and a 3D animated set featuring characters from *Rayman Raving Rabbids™*.
  - **Updated Chess Engine**: Enhanced multithread and multiprocessor capabilities.
 
---

### **Installation and Setup**
- **Installation Requirements**:
  - Minimum space: 1.2 GB; full installation: 2.0 GB.
  - Use the setup program on Disc 1 to install; manual copying won't work.
- **First-Time Setup**:
  - Create your player profile ("alter ego") by providing a name, age, and chess experience level to determine your starting rating.
- **Uninstallation**:
  - Insert the installation disc and select "Uninstall" or use the shortcut in the Windows program menu.

### **Game Modes**
#### **Play Modes**
1. **Training Mode**:
   - Immediate gameplay against AI.
   - Allows undoing moves and receiving hints.
2. **Ranked Play Mode**:
   - "For real" chess matches where your rating changes after every game.
   - No hints or advice tools allowed.
3. **Set Up Position**:
   - Create specific board setups to solve puzzles or replicate famous games.
4. **Tournaments**:
   - Participate in over 50 predefined tournaments or create your own.
5. **Online Play**:
   - Play on ubi.com servers with chat, rated matches, ladders, and simultaneous exhibitions.
6. **LAN Play**:
   - Local network play similar to Online Mode but without clans or ladders.

#### **Learn Modes**
1. **Academy**:
   - Features tutorials, drills, and quizzes for beginners, intermediates, and advanced players.
   - Includes content from renowned chess players like John Nunn and Larry Evans.
2. **Openings**:
   - A training mode for chess openings, allowing practice, reference, editing, or creating opening books.
3. **Famous Games Database**:
   - Watch and analyze 825 famous chess games (1619–2007) with commentary.
4. **Comprehensive Database**:
   - Browse almost 800,000 games with advanced search tools (e.g., fastest games, pawn wars, etc.).

#### **Fun Modes**
1. **Play Mode**: Simplified chess for kids and beginners using animated sets.
2. **Puzzles**: Solve unlimited chess puzzles with types like Mate in X moves.
3. **Mini-Games**:
   - **Fork My Fruit**: Practice forks.
   - **Minefield**: Avoid hidden mines while moving chess pieces.
   - **Pathfinder**: Capture pieces while growing a "tail."

### **Quick Guide to Chessmaster**
1. **Interface Basics**:
   - Resize and rotate the chessboard using the mouse or arrow keys.
   - Right-click for contextual menus in every mode.
   - Use shortcuts and contextual help for efficient navigation.
2. **Navigation Bar**:
   - Switch between Play, Learn, and Fun categories quickly.
   - Access game modes within each category via drop-down arrows.
3. **Gameplay Features**:
   - Use "Memo Board" to experiment with alternative moves.
   - Analyze games with tools like "Post-Game Analysis" and "Game Chart."
   - Access detailed advice via the Chess Coach (e.g., highlights for legal moves, danger zones, pinned pieces).

---

### **Advanced Features**
#### **Advice Tools**
1. **Quick Hint**: Highlights the target square or suggests moves.
2. **Chess Coach**: Offers detailed visual hints (moves, captures, danger zones).
3. **Blunder Alert**: Alerts for potential mistakes, with options to undo moves.
4. **Analysis Mode**: Tests hypothetical moves during gameplay.

#### **Saving and Loading Games**
- Games can be saved and restored in PGN format.
- Adjourn unfinished games in Ranked Play and reload them later.

#### **Customization**
- Modify layouts, backgrounds, chess sets (2D/3D), and interface skins.
- Personalize your avatar by replacing image files in the game directory.

### **Online Play**
1. **Account Setup**:
   - Requires a ubi.com account to play online.
   - Children under 13 need parental approval.
2. **Playing Online**:
   - Access lobbies to find opponents, chat, and spectate matches.
   - Play Correspondence Chess, participate in tournaments, or join clan activities.
3. **Special Modes**:
   - Create or join ladders, clans, and simultaneous exhibitions.
   - Utilize teaching games to mentor or learn from others.

#### **Clans and Leagues**
- Clans compete in yearly leagues, with points awarded for various outcomes.
