### **1. Game Modes**
- **Play Now**: Quick matches against CPU or another player. Results/stats are saved to your profile. Enables unlocking **Madden Cards**.
- **Online Play**: Competitive play against other players online. Focus on vying with skilled opponents.
- **Tournament Mode**: Supports up to 16 players with formats like Double Elimination, Single Elimination, or Round Robin.
- **Mini-Games**:
  - **Rushing Attack**: Improves stick skills for running and tackling.
  - **Two-Minute Drill**: Simulates high-pressure endgame situations.
- **Practice Mode**: Allows testing of plays against various defenses.
- **Fantasy Challenge**: Create and manage a fantasy draft team to compete at multiple difficulty levels.

---

### **2. Key Features**
- **Auto Motion**: Pre-scripted player movement that boosts offensive opportunities.
- **Receiver Control**: Allows manual control of receivers to customize routes and positions.
- **Player Weapons**:
  - Introduces 26 Player Weapon categories like "Shutdown Corner," "Cannon Arm," and "Power Back."
  - Use Player Weapons Cam to view matchups on the field.
- **Hit Stick 2.0**: Adds high or low hit mechanics for enhanced defensive strategies.

---

### **3. Offense Strategies**
#### **Running Game**
- **Running Moves**:
  - **Cover Up**: Protects the ball to prevent fumbles.
  - **Spin/Juke Moves**: Break tackles or avoid defenders.
  - **Stiff Arm**: Pushes defenders aside.
  - **Speed Burst**: Boosts acceleration but reduces stamina.
  - **Highlight Stick**: Executes animations based on the player's attributes (e.g., power or finesse moves).
  
- **Inside Runs**:
  - Plays like **HB Slam** and **FB Dive** excel in breaking defenses down the middle.
- **Outside Runs**:
  - **Toss and Stretch Plays** provide excellent perimeter runs.
  
- **Misdirection Plays**:
  - Trick defenses with fake handoffs like the **HB Toss Fake FB Dive.**
- **Draws and Delays**:
  - Exploit pass-heavy defenses with plays like **HB Draw.**

---

#### **Passing Game**
- **QB Vision Cone**: Enhances passing accuracy by directing focus to a primary receiver.
- **QB Precision Placement**:
  - Four directional passes: **High**, **Low**, **Inside**, and **Outside**.
  - High passes excel against man-coverage; low passes avoid interceptions.
- **Hot Routes**:
  - Modify receiver routes pre-snap (e.g., Streak, Curl, Slant).
  - Use strategically to counter defensive setups.
- **Smart Routes**:
  - Adjust route lengths dynamically based on first-down requirements.
- **Slide Protection**:
  - Adjust blocking schemes to counter blitz-heavy defenses.

---

### **4. Defense Strategies**
- **Hit Stick 2.0**:
  - Aim high or low to increase the chance of fumbles.
  - Effective against ball carriers of varying sizes.
- **Smart Zones**:
  - Assign defenders to specific zones to protect critical areas like first-down markers.
- **Receiver Spotlight**:
  - Focus on dangerous receivers during pre-snap and post-snap.

---

### **5. Customization Features**
- **Create-A-Player/Team/Fan**:
  - Design unique players, new teams with custom logos, and even team fans for cheering sections.
- **Create-A-Playbook**:
  - Build custom playbooks by creating formations and plays.

### **6. Franchise and NFL Superstar Mode**
- **Franchise Mode**:
  - Manage every facet of a team, including drafting, training, and financial decisions across 30 seasons.
- **NFL Superstar Mode**:
  - Follow a created player's journey from college to the NFL Hall of Fame.

### **10. Key Study Points**
1. Understand all Player Weapon categories and their in-game uses.
2. Memorize the distinctions of **Auto Motion**, **Receiver Control**, and **Precision Placement.**
3. Be able to explain various passing and rushing strategies (e.g., Smart Routes, Highlight Stick).
4. Detail Franchise Mode's mechanics, including drafting and financial management.
5. Recall gameplay-customization options, like Slide Protection, Hot Routes, and Create-A-Playbook.
6. Distinguish between game modes and their unique features (e.g., Two-Minute Drill vs. Practice Mode).
7. Recognize defensive strategies, including **Hit Stick 2.0**, **Smart Zones**, and **Receiver Spotlight.**
8. Identify differences between inside and outside running games and applicable plays.
