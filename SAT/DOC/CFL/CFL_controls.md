## Offensive Play Commands

### **Coach Cam Art**
- **Buttons**:
  - **Real Play Art**: Right Thumbstick Up (8)
  - **Bluff Pass Play**: Right Thumbstick Right (6)
  - **Bluff Run Play**: Right Thumbstick Down (2)
  - **Bluff Play Action Pass**: Right Thumbstick Left (4)
- **Function**: Display true or fake offensive play art to mislead opponents, particularly in local multiplayer.

---

### **Receiver Control**
- **Buttons**:
  - **Pre-Snap Control**: Press `B` (Xbox) / `O` (PS2) pre-snap to cycle through eligible receivers.
  - **Call for Ball**: Press Left Trigger (`LT` on Xbox) / (`L1` on PS2) post-snap.
- **Function**: Manually run the route of a targeted receiver and request the ball.

---

### **Hot Routes**
- **Buttons**:
  - **Streak**: Press Hot Route + Up D-Pad (8).
  - **Curl**: Press Hot Route + Down D-Pad (2).
  - **Out/In**: Press Hot Route + Right (6) or Left D-Pad (4).
  - **Slant/Fade/Flat/Drag**: Hot Route + Right Thumbstick in various directions.
- **Function**: Adjust receiver routes before the snap.

---

### **QB Vision Cone**
- **Buttons**:
  - **Activate Vision Cone**: Press Right Trigger (`RT` on Xbox / `R2` on PS2).
  - **Move the Cone**: Use Right Thumbstick to aim or press the receiver’s button to lock on.
- **Function**: Lock onto receivers or lead passes with precision.

---

### **Slide Protection**
- **Buttons**: Hold Left Trigger (`LT`) + D-Pad Direction.
  - **Left**: Slide Left (4).
  - **Right**: Slide Right (6).
  - **Aggressive**: Slide Up (8).
  - **Conservative**: Slide Down (2).
- **Function**: Shift the offensive line to counter specific defensive pressure.

---

## Defensive Play Commands

### **Coach Cam Art**
- **Buttons**:
  - **Real Play Art**: Right Thumbstick Up (8).
  - **Bluff Man Coverage**: Right Thumbstick Right (6).
  - **Bluff Blitz**: Right Thumbstick Down (2).
  - **Bluff Zone Coverage**: Right Thumbstick Left (4).
- **Function**: Similar to offensive cam but used to bluff defensive setups.

---

### **Hit Stick 2.0**
- **Buttons**:
  - **High Hit**: Flick Right Thumbstick Up (8).
  - **Low Hit**: Flick Right Thumbstick Down (2).
- **Function**: Deliver high or low hits based on ball carrier size.

---

### **Receiver Spotlight**
- **Buttons**:
  - **Activate Spotlight**: Push Right Thumbstick (`R3`) + Press Receiver's Button.
- **Function**: Focus defenders on a specific receiver to tighten coverage.

---

### **Smart Zones**
- **Buttons**: Press Right Thumbstick Up Twice.
- **Function**: Adjust defenders to focus on first-down or goal-line coverage.

---

## Running Controls

### **Running Moves**
- **Cover Ball**: Hold Right Bumper (`RB` Xbox / `R1` PS2) to lower shoulders and protect against fumbles.
- **Spin Move**: Press `O` (PS2) / `B` (Xbox).
- **Juke**: Flick Right Thumbstick Left/Right.
- **Stiff Arm**: Press Left/Right Bumper based on direction.
- **Speed Burst**: Hold Right Trigger (`RT` Xbox) / (`R2` PS2).

---

### **Highlight Stick**
- **Buttons**:
  - **Activate**: Flick Right Thumbstick Up (8) for larger backs or finesse moves for smaller backs.
- **Function**: Trigger animations to overpower or evade defenders based on the ball carrier’s attributes.
