Datalink Overview
-----------------

*   Definition: A digital system that provides position information of other aircraft in your flight and package
*   Purpose: Increases situational awareness and allows digital transmission of cursor and positional data

Benefits of Datalink
--------------------

1.  Improved situational awareness
2.  Reduced communication clutter
3.  Quick sorting and assignment of air contacts
4.  Digital transmission of cursor and position data
5.  Sharing of mark points and steer points

Hosting the Datalink
--------------------

*   Default: Datalink is not hosted automatically
*   Steps to host:
    
    1.  Change "Assign" option on left FCR to "CONT" (continuous)
    2.  Hold CTRL + O for about 2 seconds
    3.  "Continuous" option will light up white
    4.  Other aircraft in your flight will appear on HSD
    

Adding Aircraft to Datalink
---------------------------

1.  Click "List" on ICP
2.  Press "Enter" on DED
3.  Click "Sequence" on ICP rocker to reach Interflight Datalink
4.  Use ICP rocker to select empty positions (5-8 recommended for other flights)
5.  Type flight numbers (e.g., 11, 21, 31) and press "Enter"
6.  Aircraft will appear on HSD and FCR

**Flight Numbering System:**

*   First flight: 11, 12, 13, 14
*   Second flight: 21, 22, 23, 24
*   Third flight: 31, 32, 33, 34
*   Fourth flight: 41, 42, 43, 44

Assigning Air Contacts
----------------------

1.  Lock target on FCR
2.  Select number (1-4) on right side of FCR
3.  "Transmit" will appear briefly
4.  Assigned number and altitude will show on FCR
5.  HSD will display assigned target

Sharing Ground Contacts
-----------------------

1.  Use air-to-ground radar or HSD
2.  Slew to desired position
3.  Press Right CTRL + P
4.  "Transmit" will appear on right side of FCR
5.  Other players will see a yellow star with your number

Transmitting Steer Points and Mark Points
-----------------------------------------

1.  Change sensor of interest to HSD (right MFD)
2.  Select desired steer point
3.  Press Right CTRL + P
4.  Others will see "Mark point" and a number on their HUD
5.  Mark points start at 71 and increment

**Accessing Steer Points:**

*   Click "Steer point" on ICP
*   Type desired steer point number
*   Press "Enter"

Creating Mark Points
--------------------

1.  Press "Mark" on ICP
2.  Select sensor (FCR, HUD, targeting pod, or current position)
3.  Slew to desired location
4.  Press TMS up to create mark point
5.  Switch to created mark point
6.  Change sensor of interest to HSD
7.  Press Right CTRL + P to transmit

**Mark Point Creation Methods:**

*   Using FCR: Slew to contact and press TMS up
*   Using Overfly: Fly over target and press TMS up

Key Commands
------------

*   CTRL + O: Host Datalink
*   Right CTRL + P: Transmit position/mark point
*   TMS up: Create mark point

Important Notes
---------------

*   Flight lead should typically host the Datalink
*   Use positions 5-8 for other flights to avoid confusion
*   Mark points appear as blue X on HSD
*   Quick steer point access: "Steer point" on ICP, type number, press "Enter"
