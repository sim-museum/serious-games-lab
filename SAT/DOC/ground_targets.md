Bombing Modes and Their Functions
---------------------------------

1.  **CCIP (Constantly Computed Impact Point)**
    
    *   **Purpose:** Visual bombing mode ideal for moving targets (e.g., tanks, vehicles).
    *   **Altitudes:** Best below 10,000 ft. If above 10,000 ft, it defaults to CCRP.
    
2.  **CCRP (Constantly Computed Release Point)**
    
    *   **Purpose:** Radar-aided bombing mode for pre-planned/static targets like buildings or facilities.
    *   **Altitudes:** Works best at 15,000 ft or higher; allows for safer standoff bombing.
    
3.  **Dive Toss (Detoss)**
    
    *   **Purpose:** A loft bombing mode used to avoid flying over dangerous targets by releasing bombs at an upward angle. Ideal for SAM sites or areas covered by AAA.
    *   **Release Angle:** Typically 45°, configurable through the SMS page.

**General Setup**
-----------------

1.  **Activate Master Air-to-Ground Mode:**
    
    *   Press **AG** on the ICP or keyboard (**Enter** or **Backspace**).
    *   Optional: Bind this function to a joystick button for convenience.
    
2.  **Arm the Master Arm:**
    
    *   Flip the **Master Arm switch** to the up/armed position.
    

**CCRP Bombing Mode**
---------------------

1.  **Activate CCRP Mode:**
    
    *   Default mode when switching to **Air-to-Ground** in the right MFD.
    *   Alternatively, select **CCRP** via mode options on the right MFD.
    
2.  **Adjust Radar:**
    
    *   Use the left MFD (FCR – Fire Control Radar):
        
        *   **Increase/Decrease Range:** Click radar range buttons.
        *   **Switch Modes (Radar Detail):** Press **GM, EXP, DBS1, DBS2** as needed for ground mapping.
        
    
3.  **Lock Target:**
    
    *   **Move Radar Cursor:** Use the **slew controls** (TMS up to enable cursor movement).
    *   **Lock Target:** Press **TMS Up** when the cursor is over the target.
    *   **Break Lock (if needed):** Press **TMS Down**.
    
4.  **Release Bomb:**
    
    *   Hold the **Pickle Button** (release weapon button) on your joystick.
    *   Fly the aircraft into the **fall line** on the HUD and maintain level flight until release.
    

**CCIP Bombing Mode**
---------------------

1.  **Activate CCIP Mode:**
    
    *   Go to the **SMS Page** on the right MFD, then select **CCIP**.
    
2.  **HUD Symbology:**
    
    *   Look for the **PIPR Circle** (dot-in-circle) at the bottom of the fall line on the HUD.
    
3.  **Attack Procedure:**
    
    *   Move the **PIPR** visually over the target.
    *   Hold the **Pickle Button** and fly into the fall line.
    *   Avoid letting the PIPR drop below the **open bracket** on the fall line (indicates ground collision risk).
    

**Dive Toss (Detoss) Bombing**
------------------------------

1.  **Activate Detoss Mode:**
    
    *   Select **Dive-Toss** mode in the SMS Page via right MFD.
    
2.  **Designate Target:**
    
    *   Use the **Target Designation Box** (linked to the HUD's meatball indicator).
    *   Slew the box over the target, then press **Pickle** to ground-stabilize it.
    
3.  **Perform Dive Toss:**
    
    *   Follow the HUD's fall line as in CCRP.
    *   When the release cue appears (a flashing circle):
        
        *   **Pitch Up** to the configured release angle (default: 45°).
        *   Hold the angle and release the bomb.
        
    

**Additional Tips**
-------------------

*   **Switch Radar to Snowplow Mode:**
    
    *   Press the **SP** button on the left MFD to scan in front of the aircraft rather than being steerpoint-focused.
    *   Useful for detecting targets outside the planned flight path.
    
*   **Radar Modes for Target Type:**
    
    *   **GM (Ground Map):** Displays stationary objects like buildings.
    *   **GMT (Ground Moving Target):** Tracks moving vehicles.
    *   **SEA (Maritime Targets):** Specialized for targets on water.
    
*   **Verify Targets with AWACS:**
    
    *   Use "**Q** + **2**" to declare targets and avoid friendly fire.
