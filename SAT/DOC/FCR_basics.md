**Fire Control Radar (FCR) Basics**
-----------------------------------

*   **Radar Range Adjustment**:
    
    *   **Increase Range**: Use the buttons on the top left of the FCR display.
    *   **Decrease Range**: Same buttons but select a lower range setting.
    
*   **Antenna Tilt (Altitude Scan Adjustment)**:
    
    *   **Raise Antenna Tilt**: Press **F5**.
    *   **Lower Antenna Tilt**: Press **F7**.
    
*   **Radar Cursor Movement (Target Slewing)**:
    
    *   Use the **arrow keys** to move the radar cursor over targets.
    
*   **Radar Modes**:
    
    *   Switch between modes like RWS (Range While Scan) and TWS (Track While Scan) by clicking the corresponding buttons at the top of the FCR display.
    

**Target Locking**
------------------

*   **Soft Lock (TMS Up Once)**: Press **TMS Up** to lock a target in RWS or TWS mode. The target will display additional information like altitude and closure rate.
*   **Hard Lock (TMS Up Twice)**: Pressing **TMS Up** again interrogates the target for identification, such as aircraft type.

**Data Sharing**
----------------

*   To share a locked target with your flight members, press the corresponding number on the right side of the FCR (e.g., press "2" to send data to Wingman #2).

**Air-to-Air Combat**
---------------------

AIM-120 AMRAAM (Radar-Guided Missile)
-------------------------------------

1.  **Enter Air-to-Air Master Mode**: Press the button labeled "AA" or use a keybinding for air-to-air mode.
2.  **Select Weapon**: Cycle between weapons using your SMS page or by pressing "AA" again. Look for "MRM" (Medium Range Missile) on your HUD.
3.  **Lock Target**: Use FCR to locate and lock a target by pressing **TMS Up**.
4.  **Check Indexer on HUD/FCR**:
    
    *   Ensure the carrot symbol is within or below the optimal range circle for a good shot.
    
5.  **Fire Missile**: Hold down the pickle button (**Weapon Release**) for about one second.

AIM-9 Sidewinder (Heat-Seeking Missile)
---------------------------------------

1.  **Cool Seeker Head**: Click "Cool" on your SMS page or use a keybinding before engaging.
2.  **Uncage Seeker Head**: Press **U**, making it more sensitive to heat signatures.
3.  **Lock Target Visually or via Radar**:
    
    *   Use FCR to lock, or rely on tone from the seeker head when visually tracking.
    
4.  **Fire Missile**: Press pickle (**Weapon Release**) once.

**Dogfight Mode**
-----------------

*   Activate Dogfight Mode: Press **D**.
    
    *   Simplifies HUD symbology and automatically selects Sidewinder missiles or guns.
    *   Radar is turned off unless manually re-enabled by pressing **TMS Up**, which creates a "T" symbol on the HUD for locking targets.
    
*   Lock Target in Dogfight Mode:
    
    *   Place the target within your boresight and press **TMS Up** to lock it.
    

**Helmet-Mounted Cueing System (HMCS)**
---------------------------------------

1.  Enable HMCS:
    
    *   Flip the HMCS switch under the ECM panel in your cockpit to activate.
    *   Adjust brightness by toggling further right.
    
2.  Lock Targets Using HMCS:
    
    *   Look at a target and hold down **TMS Up**, then release when ready to lock.
    
3.  Disable HMCS Quickly:
    
    *   Hold down **DMS Down** to turn it off; repeat to turn it back on.
