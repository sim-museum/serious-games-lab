Activating the TGP
------------------

1.  Press the TGP MFD button to display the TGP page on one of your MFDs.
2.  Click the TGP MFD button until it shows "A-G" mode.
3.  Press DMS down to make the TGP the sensor of interest (SOI) 

Slewing the TGP
---------------

1.  With the TGP as SOI, press TMS up to enable TGP slewing
2.  Use the radar slew controls (arrow keys by default) to move the TGP cursor around.
3.  Zoom in/out on the TGP image using the FOV button on the MFD.

Locking a Target
----------------

1.  Slew the TGP cursor over the target you want to lock.
2.  Press TMS up to lock the target. The crosshairs will stop flashing when locked.

Dropping Laser Guided Bombs
---------------------------

1.  Select an LGB on the SMS page.
2.  Fly towards the target, putting the flight path marker through the vertical steering line on the HUD.
3.  Hold down the weapon release button. The bomb will release when the steering line meets the flight path marker.
4.  Keep the TGP crosshairs on the target. The laser will activate automatically ~6 seconds before impact.

Some other tips:

*   The TGP can be used in both A-A and A-G master modes.
*   In A-A mode, the TGP will automatically slew to the contact you have locked on radar, allowing visual identification.
*   You can save TGP settings like polarity, FOV, etc. in your Data Cartridge.

With practice, the TGP will become an invaluable tool for identifying and engaging ground targets with precision. The key is managing the SOI between the FCR, TGP and other sensors as needed.
