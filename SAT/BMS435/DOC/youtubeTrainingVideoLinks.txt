Note: For our purposes we can think of the BMS 4 sims as newer and more complicated versions of Free Falcon.  
Most youtube training videos are for the BMS 4 versions, but these sims are so similar to Free Falcon that 
the videos work as Free Falcon training also.

Note: The BMS versions run under linux/wine, but require purchase of the original Falcon 4 game.  See 
"Comparing Free Falcon vs BMS.pdf" in SAT/DOC for details.
