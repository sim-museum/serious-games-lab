SAT Scoring
eSports for Engineers 24041LTS

Note: save your replay files after each session.  For FreeFalcon and
Falcon BMS, these are .vhs files.  Copy your replay files, along with
screenshot .png files and debriefing files, to the SAT/afterGameReport
directory, calculate your score as described below, and then enter your
score using the launcher.

Note: these OKRs can be completed with any of the combat flight
programs available on SAT (FreeFalcon, Falcon AF, BMS 4.32, BMS 4.35, CFL).

OBJECTIVE #1: LEARN COMBAT FLIGHT SIMULATION AT AN ADVANCED LEVEL
OBJECTIVE #2: (ASPIRATIONAL) COMPLETE A FULL CAMPAIGN
OBJECTIVE #3: USER DEFINED (can be regular or aspirational)
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 1, first key result for objective "learn combat flight sim"

Complete the ramp start procedure and take off successfully in
FreeFalcon or Falcon BMS.  Use rampStartTrainer if available.

score=.1 if you completed the ramp start and took off.
score=0 otherwise.
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 2

Complete a training mission: air-to-air intercept or air-to-ground
strike.  Review the ACMI replay (.vhs file) afterward.

score=.1 if you completed the mission and reviewed the replay.
score=0 otherwise.
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 3

Study the F-16 documentation in SAT/DOC/ (basic orientation,
commands summary, tactical references).  Apply what you learned
in a training sortie.

score=.1 if completed.  score=0 otherwise.
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 4

Complete a campaign mission in Falcon BMS or FreeFalcon.  Save
the debriefing and replay files.

If mission successful: score=.1
If mission attempted but failed, and you reviewed what went wrong: score=.05
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 5

The following tasks are worth .05 points each:

Read SAT/DOC/basic_F16_orientation.md

Skim all documentation in the SAT/DOC directory

Complete the ramp start from memory without the trainer

Complete an air-to-air refueling or formation flight exercise

Use the FlightGear F-16 or MiG-21 trainer for basic jet handling
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #2, 1

Complete a full campaign (Falcon BMS or FreeFalcon).  If you
complete the campaign successfully, give yourself a score of
1 good for the next month.

If you have not completed the full campaign:
score=0.7*(missions_completed/total_missions), with maximum score of .7

Note: Your daily total is the sum of your OKR scores (subject to an
upper limit of 0.7 unless you have an aspirational OKR score above 0.7).
