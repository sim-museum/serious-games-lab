Overview of bridge bidding using the standard American yellow card (SAYC) convention
------------

Opening Bids
------------

*   Open the bidding with 12+ points
*   1♣/1♦ opening bid promises 3+ cards in the suit and 12+ points
*   1♥/1♠ opening bid promises 5+ cards in the suit and 12+ points

Balanced Hands
--------------

*   Balanced hand shapes: 4-3-3-3, 4-4-3-2, 5-3-3-2
*   Balanced if two longest suits contain 8 cards or fewer (except with a singleton)
*   Open 1NT with 15-17 HCP and a balanced hand
*   Open one-of-a-suit and rebid 1NT with 12-14 HCP and a balanced hand
*   Open one-of-a-suit and jump rebid 2NT with 18-19 HCP and a balanced hand
*   Open 2NT with 20-21 HCP and a balanced hand

Unbalanced Hands
----------------

*   Single-suited:
    
    *   Open and rebid suit at cheapest level with minimum values
    *   Open and jump rebid suit with medium values
    *   Open and jump to game with maximum values
    
*   Two-suited:
    
    *   With minimum values, open longer suit and rebid cheaper of the two suits
    *   With medium+ values, open longer suit and bid the other suit
    

Responding to Partner's Opening
-------------------------------

*   0-5 points: Pass
*   6+ points: Bid
*   Raise partner's major to the 2-level with 3+ card support and 6-9 points
*   Jump raise partner's major with 3+ card support and 10-12 points
*   Raise partner's major to game with 3+ card support and 13-15 points
*   New suit at the one-level shows 4+ cards and 6+ points
*   New suit at the two-level shows 4+ cards and 10+ points
*   1NT response shows 6-9 points without support or a biddable suit

Doubles
-------

*   Takeout double of opponent's bid shows shortage in their suit, support for unbid suits, and opening values
*   Penalty double of 4♠ or higher aims to defeat the contract
*   Redouble is very rare

General Principles
------------------

*   Bidding is used to find the best contract and a fit (8+ cards in a suit)
*   Limit your hand by bidding NT or repeating a suit to help partner judge the right level
*   Bidding new suits is unlimited and forcing, asking partner to describe their hand further
*   Aim for a game bonus by bidding to the right level with 25+ combined points
*   Open leads and defensive signals are used to convey information to partner
