TUE Scoring
eSports for Engineers 24041LTS

Note: save your replay files after each session.  For Battle of Britain,
these are .cam files in BattleOfBritain/WP.  For MiG Alley, these are
.cam files in MigAlley/WP.  Copy your replay files, along with
screenshot .png files, to the TUE/afterGameReport directory, calculate
your score as described below, and then enter your score using the launcher.

Note: these OKRs can be completed with any of the flight simulation
programs available on TUE (Battle of Britain, FlightGear, MiG Alley,
FS9, WWI SDOE, WWII SDOE).

OBJECTIVE #1: LEARN FLIGHT SIMULATION WELL ENOUGH TO FLY MISSIONS
OBJECTIVE #2: (ASPIRATIONAL) COMPLETE A FULL CAMPAIGN
OBJECTIVE #3: USER DEFINED (can be regular or aspirational)
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 1, first key result for objective "learn flight simulation"

Complete a basic flight training session in FlightGear using
the Cessna (basicFlightTraining).  Practice takeoff, level flight,
turns, and landing.

score=.1 if you completed a training session and can perform
basic maneuvers.  score=0 otherwise.
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 2

Fly one of the warbird trainers in FlightGear (Spitfire, Me 109,
P-51D, F-86, or MiG-15).  Complete a circuit and land successfully.

score=.1 if you completed the flight and landed successfully.
score=0 otherwise.
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 3

Complete a mission in Battle of Britain or MiG Alley (if installed).
Review the debriefing.

score=.1 if you completed the mission.  score=.05 if you attempted
but did not complete the mission and reviewed what went wrong.
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 4

Study the documentation for one aircraft.  For Battle of Britain,
see TUE/BattleOfBritain/DOC/.  For MiG Alley, see TUE/MigAlley/DOC/.
Apply what you learned in a training session.

score=.1 if completed.  score=0 otherwise.
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 5

The following tasks are worth .05 points each:

Read the getting started guide for your simulator
(e.g., TUE/MigAlley/DOC/GettingStartedGuide_TUE.pdf)

Skim all documentation in one of the TUE DOC directories

Complete a FS9 training flight (if installed)

Study dogfighting tactics from TUE/MigAlley/DOC/dogfightingSummary.md

Complete a campaign mission in Battle of Britain or MiG Alley and
save the campaign progress
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #2, 1

Complete a full campaign (all missions) in Battle of Britain or
MiG Alley.  If you complete the campaign, give yourself a score
of 1 good for the next month.

If you have not completed the full campaign:
score=0.7*(missions_completed/total_missions), with maximum score of .7

Note: Your daily total is the sum of your OKR scores (subject to an
upper limit of 0.7 unless you have an aspirational OKR score above 0.7).
