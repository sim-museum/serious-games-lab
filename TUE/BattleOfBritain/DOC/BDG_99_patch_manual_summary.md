### **Part I: Installation Guide**

- **Installation Steps:** 
  - Ensure no Rowan patches are necessary. BDG patch overwrites all previous installations.
  - Use the self-installer and locate your Battle of Britain directory accurately.
  - The manual can be found under the IDocs folder after installation.
  - Post-installation, check for the BDG product line in the game's UI.

- **Manual Translation:**
  - Updated files for German, Spanish, and French. No updates for Italian and Russian.

- **Post-Installation Tips:**
  - Avoid third-party mods before patching. 
  - Check for proper path verification during installation.
  - Verify updated entries in `bdg.txt`.

### **Part II: In-Game Guide**

- **Configuration Screens:**
  - **Field of View Configuration:**
    - `s e`: Zoom to smallest FOV
    - `s r`: Zoom to medium FOV
    - `s t`: Zoom to large FOV
    - `s z`: Toggle between FOV settings
    - Settings for max/min zoom, initial FOV, and padlock targets.
  - **Game Configuration:**
    - Toggle options like 'No Friendly Collisions', 'No Engine Cutout', 'No Stall Horn', etc.
    - Enabling/disabling features like TrackIR, AutoGen, etc.

- **In-Game Features:**
  - **Padlock Centre:** Use the `S` key for targeted padlocking.
  - **WinAmp Support:** Control music with assigned keys.
  - **Autopilot:** Toggle with `c A`.
  - **Other Aircraft View:** Cycle through AI planes with `c I`.
  - **Performance Enhancements:** Smoother joystick deadzone, preloading assets, belly landing, new bullet ballistics.

- **Control Key Changes:**
  - New keys for various functions including time deceleration (`s q`), toggling views, and controlling music.
