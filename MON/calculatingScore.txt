MON Scoring
eSports for Engineers 24041LTS

Note: save screenshots and hand histories after each session.
Copy your game output files to the MON/afterGameReport directory,
calculate your score as described below, and then enter your score
using the launcher.

Note: these OKRs can be completed with any of the poker programs
available on MON (PokerTH, PokerStove, pokerIQ, generalPokerEvaluator).

OBJECTIVE #1: LEARN POKER WELL ENOUGH TO PLAY IN A HOME GAME
OBJECTIVE #2: (ASPIRATIONAL) ACHIEVE CONSISTENT POSITIVE RESULTS
OBJECTIVE #3: USER DEFINED (can be regular or aspirational)
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 1, first key result for objective "learn poker"

Play a session of at least 50 hands in PokerTH against AI
opponents.  Track your results (buy-in vs. ending stack).

score=.1 if you played 50+ hands and tracked your results.
score=0 otherwise.
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 2

Use PokerStove to analyze at least 5 hands from your session.
For each hand, calculate your equity at the decision point and
compare to your actual play.

score=.1 if you completed the analysis for 5+ hands.
score=0 otherwise.
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 3

Use pokerIQ to study one poker concept (pot odds, implied odds,
position play, hand reading).  Apply that concept in a 50-hand
session.

score=.1 if completed.  score=0 otherwise.
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 4

Play a tournament (sit-and-go or multi-table) in PokerTH with
at least 6 opponents.

If you finish in the top 3: score=.1
If you finish in the money: score=.05
If you finish out of the money but reviewed key hands: score=.025
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #1, 5

The following tasks are worth .05 points each:

Read the poker basics documentation in MON/DOC/

Study pot odds and implied odds using PokerStove

Play the Las Vegas bracelets game (if installed) and complete
one event

Use generalPokerEvaluator to analyze a specific scenario

Study one chapter from a recommended poker book or video
---------------------------------------------------------------
OKR #2, 1

Play 500+ hands in a session and achieve a positive win rate
(measured in big blinds per 100 hands).

If your win rate is positive:
score=1 good for the next month.

If your win rate is negative:
score=0.7*(1 - abs(bb_per_100)/20), with minimum score of 0,
maximum score of .7

Note: Your daily total is the sum of your OKR scores (subject to an
upper limit of 0.7 unless you have an aspirational OKR score above 0.7).
