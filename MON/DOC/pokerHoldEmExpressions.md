Fundamental Poker Terms
-----------------------

**Hand**

*   Two meanings:
    
    1.  Individual round of the game from card dealing to pot winning
    2.  Five-card holding each player has after all cards are dealt
    

**Whole Cards**

*   Two cards dealt face down to each player at the start of a Hold'em hand
*   Also known as starting hand

**Community Cards**

*   Five cards dealt face up in the middle of the table
*   Used by all players
*   Also known as the board

**Pot**

*   Total number of chips created by all bets made during a hand

**Stack**

*   Number of chips a player has available to play with

Dealing and Card Handling
-------------------------

**Deal**

*   Process of distributing cards to players in the correct order

**Shuffle**

*   Randomly ordering cards before the deal
*   Done before every hand

**Wash**

*   Shuffling method where dealer mixes face-down cards in a pile

**Burn**

*   In live poker, discarding a card from the top of the deck before dealing flop, turn, or river

Blinds and Related Terms
------------------------

**Blinds**

*   Refers to players in small blind and big blind positions or the bets themselves

**Blind Level**

*   Section of a tournament with fixed blind amounts

**Steal the Blinds**

*   Betting pre-flop to take chips contributed by blind players without being called

**Defend the Blinds**

*   Not folding from small or big blind position when there's a pre-flop raise

**Blinding Out**

*   Losing chips by posting blinds without playing hands

**Walk**

*   Big blind winning pot without action when all other players fold pre-flop

Betting Streets and Stages
--------------------------

**Street**

*   Stage of a hand with betting round after specific card(s) are dealt

**Pre-flop**

*   After whole cards dealt, before community cards

**Flop**

*   First three community cards

**Turn**

*   Fourth community card

**River**

*   Fifth and final community card

**Showdown**

*   Players reveal whole cards to determine winner

Player Actions
--------------

**Muck**

*   Not revealing cards at showdown, accepting defeat

**Action**

*   Decision a player takes on each betting street

**Bet**

*   Putting chips into the pot (first player to do so)

**Raise**

*   Increasing the size of an existing bet

**Call**

*   Matching someone else's bet without raising
*   Pre-flop, calling the big blind is known as limping

**Fold**

*   Surrendering hand and giving up on the pot

**Laydown**

*   Folding a very good hand

**Check**

*   Signal that a player doesn't want to bet

**Option**

*   Big blind's chance to check or bet when no one has raised pre-flop

**All-in**

*   Betting or raising all remaining chips
*   Also known as shoving, pushing, or jamming

Advanced Betting Terms
----------------------

**Three-bet**

*   Re-raising after an initial bet and raise

**Four-bet**

*   Raising after a three-bet

**Five-bet**

*   Raising after a four-bet

**Value Bet**

*   Betting with the best hand to build pot and win chips

**Bluff**

*   Betting with a poor hand to make others fold better hands

**Semi-bluff**

*   Bluffing with a draw (potential to improve)

**Continuation Bet (C-bet)**

*   First bet on next street by player who made final raise in previous round
*   Also known as barrelling

**Donk Bet**

*   Player who called a raise bets first on next street instead of checking to aggressor
